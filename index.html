WrightHere is a 3D-AI platform
that supports children's creative writing.

When facing writer's block, it allows users to step into their own stories.
Interact within your character’s world, and find inspiration for the narratives.

Features

Narrative Panel

Write your story paragraph by paragraph. Each written section becomes a foundation for generating corresponding 3D worlds that bring your narrative to life.

#Writing #Creative #Storytelling

Story World

Explore generated 3D environments based on your written narrative. Click on world thumbnails to dive into immersive spaces, adjust character positions, and experience your story from within.

#3D #Immersive #Exploration

Create Character

Add new characters or objects to your story world. Click any empty area, describe what you envision, and watch as the system generates and places your creation at the selected location.

#Creation #AI #Interactive

Character Dialogue

Engage in meaningful conversations with characters in your story world. Through dialogue, gain new perspectives and inspiration to develop and advance your narrative.

#Dialogue #Interaction #Inspiration

VR Version

Experience your story in immersive virtual reality. Enter fully into your created worlds and interact with your characters in a completely new dimension.

#VR #Immersive #Experience
